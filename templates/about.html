About Gweru District Hospital

Gweru District Hospital is the referral hospital in Gweru District, serving a population of over 280,000. We are committed to providing quality, accessible healthcare to all.

Our Services

  • Outpatient and Emergency services
  • Rehabilitation and physiotherapy
  • Nutrition and dietetics
  • Pharmacy and laboratory services
  • Family and child health, immunizations
  • HIV/AIDS and TB care

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, patient-centered care and continuously improve through community feedback.

The Feedback App

This application was developed by Gweru District Health Promotion Unit to gather anonymous feedback from patients, clients and visitors. Your input helps us identify strengths and areas for improvement.
